Approach

My methodology combines three pillars: mathematical models, multi-agent simulation, and experimental economics. I move back and forth between theory and reality through these approaches.

  1. Game theory

    Mathematical analysis of strategic interaction in markets and matching mechanisms.

  2. Multi-agent simulation

    Data analysis and LLM-driven agents approximate human decision-making in economic experiments. By carefully weaving simulations into the process and running economic experiments effectively, I streamline the cycle toward social implementation.

  3. Experimental economics

    Controlled human-subject studies to validate mechanism designs in the lab and in the wild.
