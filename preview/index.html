Ayato Kitadai

The University of Tokyo, Graduate School of Engineering
Department of Technology Management for Innovation · PhD Student (2nd year)

I study the design of institutions and services in the AI era, with particular emphasis on human decision-making. I value practical, engineering-oriented perspectives while also pursuing scientific rigor.

My methodology combines the following approaches.

This enables me to iterate between institutional design and impact evaluation, producing research that bridges scientific and practical value.

  • Mathematical modeling and analysis using game theory
  • Data analysis and multi-agent simulation
  • Experimental economics

Research Interests

Game Theory, Market Design, Experimental Economics, Large Language Models, Mechanism Design, Service Engineering
